Birding & combination extension tours to Cambodia, Laos & Burma

Vietnam Birding can tailor birding and birding combination tours to Cambodia, Laos and Burma. Combine your Vietnam birding trip with an extension to Cambodia to see the critically endangered Giant and White-shouldered Ibises, Bengal Florican and Mekong Dolphin and marvel at the magnificent temples of Angkor. Or head to Laos to relax in the peaceful mountain kingdom of Luang Prabang, take a cruise down the Mekong or search for the bizarre Bare-faced Bulbul in the limestone hills of central Laos. Perhaps extend your birding to include Burma, to search for the endemic Hooded Treepie and Jerdon’s Minivet and explore the archeological sites of Bagan.
