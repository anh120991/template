Welcome

Welcome to Vietnam Birding, the specialist in custom birdwatching adventures and birding combination tours to Vietnam and beyond.

Established in Ho Chi Minh City by resident birder Richard Craik, Vietnam Birding is a fully-licensed international tour operator and the expert on the ground with the local knowledge to ensure you the best possible Vietnam birding experience. With over 25 years of experience in arranging travel to Indochina, Vietnam Birding will take all the hassle out of planning your trip with a birdwatching tour tailor-made to suit your requirements.

Don’t want a total birding tour? No problem – we can combine the best of Vietnam’s birding locations with the best of Vietnam’s cultural sites, river cruising, spa resorts and beaches or extend your travels to Cambodia, Laos or Burma.

So whether you are an experienced world birder in search of Vietnam’s spectacular endemic birds or are looking for a more leisurely birding experience combined with fascinating cultural sites or some relaxation, leave it to Vietnam Birding to design the perfect tour for you. Contact us at info@vietnambirding.com with your wishes and we’ll do the rest!

Ethical birding

Vietnam Birding is an ethical birding company and we follow a strict code of ethics that always put the welfare of the birds and other wildlife and their habitats first. Click here vietnambirding.com/ethics.aspx for more.

Beware of imitators!

We should be flattered to have so many imitators but please beware of similar sounding websites with names such as “vietnambirdingtours” and “vietnambirdtours” claiming to offer birding tours in Vietnam. The organisations behind these and other similarly-named websites whose content is often lifted directly from the Vietnam Birding website are not connected in any way to Vietnam Birding, the original specialist birding tour operator in Vietnam. To be sure you are talking with the experts e-mail us at info@vietnambirding.com
